Eloquent Relationship
Relationships in laravel are called Eloquent Relationships.
This is very powerful feature in laravel to allow devs to work with related data easily.
Ex (Tables are connected with each other)

Types of Relationships
1.One to One Relationship : means one row is related to one row of another table.

2.One to many Relationship : means one row is related to multiple rows of another table.

3.Many to one Relationship : means multiple rows of one table is related to single row of
another table.
